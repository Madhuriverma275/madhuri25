My Travel Bucket List

Here are my top dream destinations I wish to visit in India!

  1. shimla

    Shimla, also known as Simla, is a captivating hill station and the capital city of Himachal Pradesh, India. It's renowned for its colonial architecture, scenic beauty, and pleasant climate, making it a popular tourist destination. Located in the foothills of the Himalayas, Shimla was the summer capital of British India and retains much of its old-world charm.

    ghumo shimla

  2. lonavala

    Lonavala is a popular hill station in the Sahyadri range of the Western Ghats, known for its scenic beauty and pleasant climate. It's a popular destination for weekend getaways from Mumbai and Pune, offering attractions like ancient caves, forts, lakes, and viewpoints. Lonavala is also famous for its chikki, a local sweet treat.

    Discover lonavala

  3. kerala

    Kerala, known as "God's Own Country", is a state in southwestern India, famed for its stunning natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and diverse tourism options. It boasts a unique landscape of palm-fringed beaches, tranquil backwaters, lush green hills, and diverse wildlife. Kerala is also a pioneer in responsible tourism and has a high literacy rate and life expectancy. .
